Our Services

Three Pillars of Institutional Placement Excellence

A structured, year-round engagement model that replaces sporadic placement drives with a consistent, results-driven pipeline.

01
External Placement Officer (EPO) Model

Ambegro embeds a dedicated External Placement Officer within your institution — a seasoned corporate recruiter who operates as an extension of your placement cell. The EPO model delivers the output of an entire placement department without institutional overhead.

Dedicated EPO assigned to campus for the full academic year
Active industry outreach and corporate relationship management
Monthly placement reports and pipeline tracking dashboards
Zero additional headcount cost for the institution
02
Tier-1 Campus Recruitment Drives

Ambegro organises and manages structured campus recruitment drives — bringing verified MNC hiring managers directly to your institution. Each drive is preceded by a 2-week corporate readiness sprint for participating students, significantly improving selection rates.

Confirmed MNC and corporate participants for every drive
Pre-drive preparation sprints to boost placement rates
Full event management, scheduling, and logistics handled
Post-drive onboarding follow-up and offer tracking
03
Strategic MOUs & Long-Term Partnerships

We formalise institutional partnerships through structured Memoranda of Understanding, creating a defined, multi-year engagement framework for placement, training, and talent development — giving institutions a strategic edge in NAAC and NBA accreditation processes.

Formal MOU framework with defined deliverables and timelines
Multi-year engagement for consistent placement outcomes
Documentation support for NAAC and NBA accreditation
Joint branding and co-branded placement event visibility
